Office Administration

Learn Your Profession with the Best Office Administration Course

By IQJITA Academy  |  February 2025

Imagine stepping into an office where you are the person in charge — keeping everything running smoothly, handling communications, organising operations, and supporting management. This dream can be your reality with the right training.

History of Office Administration

From typewriters and filing cabinets in the early 1900s to today's cloud-powered systems, the role of an office administrator has transformed into that of a strategic thinker who supports decision-making and communication at every level.

Why Choose Office Administration?

  • Strong foundation for career growth — Office admins develop skills that lead to senior management and supervisory roles
  • Transferable skills for multiple industries — Healthcare, education, logistics, retail, and finance all need skilled office administrators
  • Opportunities for promotions — Proven performers move into executive assistant and operations management roles
  • Stable professional work environment — Office administration roles offer consistent hours and a structured working environment

Who Can Join?

  • 12th pass students
  • Graduates from any stream
  • Career shifters
  • Entrepreneurs managing offices
