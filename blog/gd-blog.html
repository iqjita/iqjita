Graphic Design

From Blank Page to Brilliance: Inside a Designer's Process

By IQJITA Academy  |  February 2025

Graphic design is the art of combining visual and text elements to create effective communication. It involves planning, creativity, and technical skills to convey messages through print and digital media. If you're passionate about creativity, a career in graphic design will lead you to the right path.

Main Tools Used in Graphic Design

  • Adobe Photoshop — Photo editing, digital art, and raster-based compositions
  • Adobe Illustrator — Vector graphics, logos, and illustrations
  • Adobe InDesign — Layouts for books, magazines, brochures, and marketing materials
  • Adobe Premiere Pro — Video editing for social media and brand content
  • Adobe After Effects — Motion graphics and visual effects
  • Adobe Media Encoder — Video format conversion and export

History of Graphic Design

Graphic design's history spans millennia, beginning with prehistoric cave paintings and hieroglyphs. The invention of the printing press in the 15th century revolutionised communication, leading to mass-printed materials. Over centuries, design movements like Art Nouveau, Bauhaus, and modern advertising shaped the field. Today, the digital era brings limitless possibilities for designers in typography, animation, and branding.

Career Opportunities in Graphic Design

A diploma in Graphic Design opens doors to careers as a Graphic Designer, UI/UX Designer, Video Editor, Motion Graphics Artist, Brand Identity Specialist, Social Media Content Creator, and Freelance Designer — both in Kerala and internationally.
